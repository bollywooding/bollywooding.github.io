How it works?

Complex yet Simple! We aim to make one great movie every year with an intention of becoming the official Oscar entry from India.

Talent Sourcing

It is open for everyone and free to take part. Participants submit their work samples online. Our judges will shortlist a list of individuals that they deem fit for the movie production.

Production

A set of producers will provide finance for the movie. The budget will be decided after the selection of participants. All selected participants will have to to sign a contract for one movie.

Rewards

No upfront fee will be provided to any participant. 50% of all profits will be equally distributed among the selected participants in ratio of their effort and experience in making the movie.

Submission Guidelines

All participants are required to send their updated Resume/CV and portfolio

Actors

Guidelines for Photos
  • All the photos should be recent
  • No computer effects or retouching
  • Do not wear sunglasses or hats
  • There should be no nudity
  • File size should not be more than 2 MB
  • Photos should be in high resolution
  • All photos should be with light background
  • Head shot - size 400 x 400 pixels
  • Side profile - size 400 x 400 pixels
  • Front full profile - size 800 x 1200 pixels
Guidelines for Videos
  • The video should be uploaded on youtube and only link to be shared
  • The video should not be more than 2 years old
  • Minimum length of the video should be 2 minutes
  • Maximum length of the video should be 10 minutes
  • The video should be in good light and you should be clearly visible
  • Sound of the video and your vocals should be clearly audible
  • There should be no URLs or links on the video
  • There should be no nudity in the video


Directors/Editors/Cinematographers/Creatives

Guidelines for Video
  • The video should be uploaded on youtube and only link to be shared
  • The video should not be more than 2 years old
  • Minimum length of the video should be 5 minutes
  • Maximum length of the video should be 30 minutes
  • The video should be of good resolution
  • Sound of the video and the vocals should be clearly audible
  • There should be no URLs or links on the video
  • The Audio track should be in Hindi
  • There should be no nudity in the video
  • There should be no child or animal abuse


Writers/Lyricists/Screenplay

Guidelines for Scripts
  • First page of the script should have your name(s), phone and email
  • The script should be in English or Hindi characters
  • The document format should be Adobe PDF (.pdf)
  • The document should not be password protected
  • Minimum pages 50 and maximum pages 200 (no minimum for lyrics)
  • Page should be letter size 8.5 x 11 inches
  • Font type should be Courier New
  • Font size should be 12 point
  • Do not use images in your script
  • Do not use a lot of hyperlinks in the script
  • Slug lines should be bold and numbered consequently
  • Character Names should be centered
  • Dialogues should be centered
  • Use proper gaps between the slugs and dialogues
  • All the pages should be numbered on the top right
